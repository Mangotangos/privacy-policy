Privacy Policy Last Updated: February 27, 2026 | App: Discovery Bible Study This Privacy Policy explains how Discovery Bible Study ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here. 1. Information We Collect To create and manage your account, we collect: Email address — used for login and optional reminder notifications Password — stored in securely hashed (encrypted) form; never stored in plain text Display name — shown within study groups Device information — device brand and model, used for troubleshooting and compatibility When you use the app, we also store the following user-generated content linked to your account: Study session responses (gratitude, challenges, discoveries, obedience, prayer notes) Verse notes and verse highlights Journal entries Commitments and completion records Weekly reflections Daily check-in and streak data Study group memberships This content is spiritual and personal in nature. It is stored securely and used only to provide app functionality. 2. How We Use Your Information Account creation and authentication Allowing you to log in securely and access your data across devices Saving and syncing your study progress, notes, and journal entries Enabling group study features Sending optional reminder notifications (only if you enable them) Improving app reliability and user experience We do not use your information for advertising, analytics, or behavioral tracking. 3. Information We Do NOT Collect We do not collect: Location data Contact lists or phone numbers Photos, camera, or media files Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data Advertising identifiers (IDFA, GAID, etc.) Financial or payment information 4. Data Storage and Security Your data is stored on secure, cloud-hosted infrastructure (Railway). We use industry-standard practices including: Encrypted password storage (hashing) Secure authentication via JWT tokens HTTPS for all data transmission No security system is 100% guaranteed, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. 5. Third-Party Sharing We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. We do not use third-party advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or data brokers. Your data is only stored on our secure backend infrastructure to provide app functionality. 6. Data Retention We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, your personal data and all associated content (journal entries, notes, session responses, etc.) will be permanently deleted within 30 days. 7. Account and Data Deletion You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. You can do this directly within the app: Go to Settings → Delete Account Confirm deletion — your account and all associated data will be permanently removed Alternatively, you may contact us at paulskc44@gmail.com with subject line "Delete My Account" and we will process the request within 30 days. This action is irreversible. 8. Children's Privacy Discovery Bible Study is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not allow users under 16 to create accounts or use the app. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16. If we become aware that a user under 16 has created an account, we will immediately delete their account and all associated data. If you believe an underage user has registered, please contact us at paulskc44@gmail.com so we can take action promptly. 9. Your Rights You have the right to: Access the personal data we hold about you Request correction of inaccurate data Request deletion of your data and account Withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use of the app To exercise any of these rights, contact us at paulskc44@gmail.com. 10.
